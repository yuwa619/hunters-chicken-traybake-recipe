HUNTER’S CHICKEN TRAYBAKE

This is an absolute air-fryer wonder—perfect for busy midweek nights. Tender chicken with bacon, peppers, sweet barbecue sauce and a cheesy finish. Great with fresh garden peas and extra BBQ sauce for dipping.

Recipe Information

  • Preparation time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking time: 23 minutes
  • Calories: 549 kcal per serving
  • Serves: 2
  • Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 350g baby potatoes, halved
  • Non-stick spray oil
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 2 bacon medallions
  • ½ red pepper, chopped
  • ½ yellow pepper, chopped
  • 4 tablespoons barbecue sauce
  • 60g mozzarella and Cheddar cheese, grated
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 180°C.
  2. Line the basket and add the baby potatoes. Season and spray with oil.
  3. Season chicken with paprika, salt, and pepper; spray with oil.
  4. Place chicken among potatoes; cook 10 minutes at 180°C.
  5. Add bacon over chicken; cook 8 minutes at 180°C.
  6. Add peppers; cook briefly.
  7. Add barbecue sauce and cheese; cook 5 minutes to melt.

Notes & Serving

  • Serve with peas or a salad.
  • Use similar-sized chicken breasts for even cooking.

Source: How to Make Anything in an Air Fryer by Hayley Dean.
